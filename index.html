Welcome to the home of JiGJAs

JiGjAS is a Melbourne-based soul jazz quartet built around the warm, gritty sound of the HAMMOND ORGAN. Along with Adrian Whitehead on Hammond Organ, the band features Gideon Brazil (tenor saxophone), Jim Green (guitar), and Sonja Horbelt (drums). Together, they craft infectious grooves inspired by the classic 1960s soul jazz era, blending deep swing, bluesy licks, and tight improvisation. With rich textures, irresistible rhythm, and a fresh take on vintage sounds, JigJas delivers high-energy sets that move both heart and feet. Perfect for lovers of groove, jazz, and everything in between.
